📋 Pitch Deck Structure (9 Slides)

  1. Title Slide — name and tagline
  2. Problem — what pain you solve
  3. Solution — your product or service
  4. Market — size and growth dynamics
  5. Business Model — how you make money
  6. Competitors — your unique advantage
  7. Team — who's building the project
  8. Financials — forecasts and key metrics
  9. Call to Action — what you need from the investor

💡 Tips for a Successful Pitch

  • 🔥 First 10 seconds — hook the investor
  • 📊 Use numbers and specific facts
  • 🎯 Show traction and growth
  • 💪 Focus on benefits, not features
  • ⏱️ Keep it to 5-7 minutes
  • 📈 End with a strong call to action
